## Thoth Alpha Search

Thoth Alpha is a computational search engine powered by AI. Ask it anything:

- **Physics:** "What is the Schwarzschild radius of the Sun?"
- **Math:** "Solve the integral of e^(-x^2) from 0 to infinity"
- **Data:** "Compare GDP growth rates of BRICS nations"
- **CS:** "Visualize a red-black tree insertion"

Results include explanations, tables, charts, interactive simulations, and executable code.
